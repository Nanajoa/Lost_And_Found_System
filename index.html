Frequently asked questions

Is Ayera free to use?

Yes, Ayera is completely free to use for both reporting found items and claiming items.

How does Ayera protect my privacy?

We never share your personal contact information publicly. Your details are sent to the person who you claimed had your item(s). The details sent are your Ashesi Email Address, Phone number, and your Name.

What areas does Ayera cover?

Ayera is available and can be used anywhere on Ashesi Campus and surrounding OffCampus Hostels and facilities.
